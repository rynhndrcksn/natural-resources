Pre-requisites and Transcripts

Step 1
Requirements:
  • Complete the pre-requisite courses: Engl& 101, Cmst& 220, Engl 128 and Math 070
  • Have a minimum college-level cumulative GPA of 2.5
  • Submit Official Transcripts (in original sealed envelopes) for all non-Green River courses to The Office of the Registrar
  • Transcript Evaluation Request form must then be submitted with "Official Transcripts" (sealed in original envelopes) to the Registrar prior to applying to the NATRS AAS.
  • All unofficial transcripts (including Green River if applicable)–Even though you may have submitted official transcripts to the Registrar, you still need to submit unofficial transcripts with this packet. These can be photocopies.
Submission:
  • Upload a PDF file of your Degree Audit
  • Upload all unofficial transcripts (can be photocopies)

Personal Statement

Step 2
Requirements:
  • Outline why joining our program will help you reach your educational/career goals. This statement does not have to be lengthy (we suggest 1-2 pages). Without the letter of interest/personal statement submitted, your application file will be deemed incomplete and not reviewed once the committee meets to assess applications.
  • Statement read and scored from 1-10 points with all reviewing committee scores averaged
Submission:
  • Upload a file (Word or PDF file) of your scored personal statement

Professional Reference Evaluation Form

Step 3
Requirements:
  • For a supervisor, manager, instructor or coach (past or present) to evaluate you on your skills. It must come directly from them, to me, either via email or to my address below. If this form comes through you, it will be rejected. Once we record the evaluation form in your file, you may request to see it.
  • Begin by downloading and printing the following document: Professional Reference Evaluation Form
Submission:
  • Upload a file (Word or PDF file) of your evaluation form

Video, Volunteer Event, or Immersion Event

Step 4
Requirements:
  • Option 1: Submit a 3-5 min video
    • Include video instructions and video upload instructions in a dropdown if they choose to do the video option
  • Option 2: Complete a volunteer event within the natural resources field
    • You will have to include a 1-3 page write up regarding the event, what you completed and what you learned. Please include at least one photo of yourself participating in the event. (Examples: WTA trail clearing event, stream restoration event with a city or county entity, volunteering at a fish hatchery, etc.)
  • Option 3: Completion of a GRC NATRS Immersion Event (subject to school approval due to COVID-19)
    • The Immersion Events consist of a 6 hour on campus Q & A about the program/history/student resources/student expectations followed by a trail hike throughout the campus onsite forest or an on-campus work project (invasive species removal, trail clearing project, wood stacking, hazard tree assessment, etc).
